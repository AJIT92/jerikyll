Hello! This year, I have decided to make a Mega Man Legends game for the C.A.V.E. system!. I was thinking which part of Mega Man Legends should I remake, then I decided to go back to the very first battle with Tron's Ferdinand (Feldynaught)!. It took me about 2-3 weeks (night time and weekends) to make this. I hope you enjoy!

It was fun to play inside the C.A.V.E., you'll really feel that you're there fighting with a giant robot! Not only that, you get to see your creation come to life!
